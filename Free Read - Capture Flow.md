# The Free Read — Capture Flow

*The spine of the machine. A free read that lands the "wholeheartedly me," then lets the price register — value first, price last, no wall, no hustle. Altruistic voice throughout.*

---

## The law it's built on

**Capture → Familiarity → Price.** They feel the value *first.* The price appears only *after* they're captured — so it registers as a fair trade for something they already want, not as a barrier. Never lead with price. Never gate. *Pull.*

---

## The structure (5 beats, in order)

1. **The Capture** — open with their profile name + the single sharpest "how did you know that" line. One sentence. Hook the ear.
2. **The Mirror** — 2–4 sentences of the overview. How their mind actually works. They go *"yes — that's me."*
3. **The Gift** — name one or two real strengths. Generous. This is the part you can't help giving — give it freely.
4. **The Phrase That Hits Home** — the screenshot line. Peak capture. The thing they send to someone who *gets* them. (This is what spreads.)
5. **The Pull** — *only now,* captured and familiar, the deeper read appears, and the price lands soft. (Copy below — reusable for every profile.)

Beats 1–4 are pure value. The price lives in beat 5 and nowhere else.

## How to run it (from your phone, your safe place)

1. **Post** something true — a mission line, an insight, or *"Answer 3 questions, I'll show you how your mind works → [free read]."*
2. They answer 3 → you send their **free read** (beats 1–4 from their profile).
3. The read **ends in the Pull** — the deep version, priced after the value.
4. The **link does the rest.** You don't ask. You don't close. You already gave; the door is just open.

You stay in your frame — *talking about the thing you believe in* — start to finish. The price never touches your mouth.
