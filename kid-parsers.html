The 27 Kid Parsers

Every kid takes in the world a little differently — not in what they like or believe, but in what they notice first. That first notice is their lens. Here are all 27.

Think of it like a camera. Some kids use a close-up lens — they see what's right here, in detail. Some use a wide-angle lens — they see the whole big picture. And some have an adjustable lens that zooms in and out. None is better. They're just different ways of seeing.

Which Parser is your kid?

The Kid Assessment finds your child's lens — and gives you language for the way they see, learn, and grow.
