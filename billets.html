Billets — seat analysis.

Billets reads the role, not the person. It decodes what a job actually demands architecturally, so you can see where the seat and the work have drifted apart before you hire into the gap.

  • Pulls the real architectural requirements out of a job description
  • Surfaces mismatches between stated qualifications and the actual work
  • Generates interview questions that reveal processing style
  • Checks the hiring team's own blind spots
  • Decodes credentials into the architecture behind them

No candidate assessment required. Billets analyses the seat; candidates are never asked to take anything.
