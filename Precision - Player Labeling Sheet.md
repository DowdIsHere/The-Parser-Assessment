## The one rule that makes this science
**Label blind to the result.** Tag a player from how he *plays* — the discriminator below — **not** from whether he wins, and **not** from any stat on this page. That's why there are **no numbers next to the names here.** If we let the clutch grips sit beside the names while you tag, you'd unconsciously label the curve instead of the player, and the whole test collapses. Identity first. Verdict second. Always in that order.

## The two types (tag only these — everything else is "—")

**F = Visionary / Future** — computes where the point is going; *tests shots to document responses,* then tries to *lead;* first-strike, takes time away, redirects, dictates. Banks the opponent's tells to **take** the heated moment.

**Pda = Past / defensive-aggressive Legacy** — lived recognition of patterns; pressure-tests through *defense* — absorbs, retrieves, makes you hit one more, reads how you crack. Counterpunches off the proven shape. Banks tells to **outlast** the heated moment.

Leave **Conf** as H / M / L (how sure your eye is). Put the actual *tell you saw* in Notes — that's the gold.
